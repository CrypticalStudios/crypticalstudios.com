  • Ethereum Improvement Proposal

    25th April, 2018

    A class of hypothetical, malevolent contracts exist which threaten the stability of the Ethereum blockchain by creating incentives to cause repeat hard forks (The Bottom Red Line, (2018)). We are proposing a system that will enable the Ethereum community to permanently freeze these contracts.
